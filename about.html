But like, what is this?

Fake News Media is a website dedicated to your opinion. F.N.M. is the "choose your own adventure" of political narratives. Armed with a complete guide to network bias, you'll have the ability to dive into whatever echo chamber you wish. For the exceedingly adventurous, you can even read across the aisle on corresponding headlines.

What is "spin?"

Spin is a biased interpretation of the facts intended to persuade your opinion for or against someone or something. Spin often implies the use of disingenuous, deceptive and highly manipulative tactics to drive an agenda.

How do you know?

Do your homework! You don't need to draft a 500 page manifesto on if your cat actually loves you (they don't, get a dog). But simply reading what the other side is saying can help you understand the topic, the facts and if this is being reported with an agenda.
